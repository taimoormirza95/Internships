Summer Internship Program (SIP)

The Summer Internship Program is a project-based internship initiative at Ufone designed to help interns work under supervision of our core teams. Being our flagship internship program, this is opened only once every summer, where only a limited number of students are accepted after intense competition. The supervisors give particular attention to provide necessary mentoring and handholding to the interns with a view to build and enhance their skills. The major highlights of the program are on-campus assessment tests, on-campus interviews, and project based internships. This intern pool helps line mangers to benefit from added manpower, while more accurately assessing candidates for future business needs. The standard duration of this project based internship program is 4-6 weeks.

ACCA / CIMA Internship Program)

The purpose of ACCA & CIMA Internship Program is to ensure an effective and efficient method of recruitment and selection of candidates having a background of ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants) and CIMA (Chartered Institute of Management Accountants). Ufone, being a certified employer by ACCA/CIMA supports the educational and experience requirement stipulated by the institutions.
